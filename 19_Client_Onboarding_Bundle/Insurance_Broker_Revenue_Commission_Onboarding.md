# Insurance Broker Onboarding — Revenue & Commission Pack

## Minimum data (to go live)
1) Monthly commission statement export (CSV/Excel) for the last 6–12 months
2) Adviser list (names exactly as used in statements)
3) Provider list (names exactly as used in statements)

## Recommended (improves leakage detection)
4) Policy register export (policy number, status, adviser, provider, premium if available)
5) Expected commission logic (if you already have it, we can map it)

## File naming (example)
- /Exports/Commission/Commission_YYYYMM.csv
- /Exports/Policies/Policies.xlsx
- /Exports/Masters/Advisers.xlsx
- /Exports/Masters/Providers.xlsx

## First outcome we target (fast ROI)
Within the first cycle:
- Total commission trend
- Provider/adviser contribution
- Missing ongoing commission list (signals)
- Unmatched / duplicate / reversal lines list

## What we will NOT do
- Product recommendations
- “advice” outputs
- compliance determinations

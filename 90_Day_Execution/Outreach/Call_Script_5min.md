“I help brokerages detect missing commission and mismatches using a repeatable Power BI management pack.”

- Do you have monthly commission statement exports (CSV/Excel)?

- Adviser + provider lists?

“Pack A gives you trend + reconciliation + missing ongoing signals. Expected vs Actual is an upgrade if we have premium/policy.”

## 4:30–5:00 — Next step
- Get sample exports (one month)
- Schedule demo review
- Proposal + timeline

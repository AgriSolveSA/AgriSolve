# Logistics / Transport KPIs

## Core KPIs
- Revenue per Trip
- Cost per Trip
- Gross Margin per Trip
- Utilisation %
- Fuel Cost %

## Trend KPIs
- Trips per Day
- Revenue per Vehicle
- Cost per KM

## Risk KPIs
- Fuel cost spike
- Underutilised vehicles
- Margin compression

## Sources
- Job/Trip export
- Fuel transactions
- Vehicle master

## Exclusions
- Route optimisation
- Telematics integrations

# Accounting / SME Financial KPIs

## Core KPIs (Executive Page)
- Revenue: Sum of GL accounts classified as Revenue
- Gross Margin: Revenue – Cost of Sales
- Gross Margin %: Gross Margin / Revenue
- Net Profit: Revenue – All Expenses
- Cash Balance: Bank account closing balance
- Debtors Balance: Sum of outstanding AR

## Trend KPIs
- Revenue MTD / YTD
- Gross Margin % MoM
- Expense Growth %

## Risk / Exception KPIs
- Debtors >60 days (% and value)
- Margin drop >10% MoM
- Expense category growth >15% MoM

## Source Tables
- GL Export
- AR Ageing
- Bank Export
- Chart of Accounts mapping

## Explicit Exclusions
- Forecasting
- Scenario modelling
- Payroll analytics (unless separate SOW)

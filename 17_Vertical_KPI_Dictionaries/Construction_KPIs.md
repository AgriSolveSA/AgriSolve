# Construction / Projects KPIs

## Core KPIs
- Revenue per Project
- Cost to Date
- Margin to Date
- % Completion (financial)
- Cash In vs Cash Out

## Risk KPIs
- Cost overruns
- Slow-paying projects
- Margin erosion

## Sources
- Project cost export
- Invoicing export
- Bank transactions

## Exclusions
- Detailed scheduling
- Earned value analysis (unless quoted)

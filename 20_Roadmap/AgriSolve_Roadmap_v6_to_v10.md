# Vantix Roadmap: v6 → v10
This roadmap converts the current toolkit into a durable, licensable operating system.
Each version adds leverage without increasing support load.

---

## Guiding Rules (Non‑Negotiable)
- Never remove or rewrite earlier content
- Every addition must:
  - reduce support
  - increase price power
  - improve repeatability
- If it adds chaos, it does not ship

---

## v6.1 (Current) — Strategic Visibility
**Purpose:** Know exactly where this is going so you don’t overbuild.

Deliverables:
- Explicit roadmap (this document)
- Versioning discipline established

Exit condition:
- You can explain the business in 2 minutes to a banker or partner.

---

## v7 — Vertical Demo Assets
**Purpose:** Sell without building.

Add:
- Read‑only demo PBIX per vertical (Accounting, Logistics, Construction)
- Sample anonymised datasets
- “What you see is what you get” screenshots

Risk reduction:
- Stops custom demo builds
- Improves close rate

---

## v8 — Automation & Validation
**Purpose:** Prevent silent failure.

Add:
- Power Query validation checks (row counts, null checks)
- Refresh health dashboard
- Automated email alerts (client‑hosted)

Risk reduction:
- Fewer emergency messages
- Faster issue isolation

---

## v9 — Partner & Licensing Layer
**Purpose:** Scale without selling 1,000 SMEs yourself.

Add:
- White‑label partner pack
- Licensing terms for template usage
- Partner onboarding checklist
- Revenue‑share models

Risk reduction:
- Partners enforce standards
- You support fewer direct clients

---

## v10 — Vantix Operating System™
**Purpose:** This becomes IP, not just a service.

Add:
- Formal methodology document
- Certification checklist (“Vantix‑compliant dashboard”)
- Annual license option
- Audit & upgrade path

End state:
- You sell access to a system, not hours
- You can pause onboarding without revenue collapsing

---

## Personal Sanity Rule
If a proposed feature:
- increases meetings,
- increases bespoke logic,
- increases client dependency,

…it is deferred or killed.

---

## Final Note
v6 is already usable.
v7–v10 are **optional upgrades**, not prerequisites.
You move forward only when cashflow and energy allow it.

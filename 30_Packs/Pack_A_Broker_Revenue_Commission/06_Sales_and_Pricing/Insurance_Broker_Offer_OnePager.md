# Vantix — Broker Revenue & Commission Pack (One Pager)

## Why brokers buy this
Commission leakage is invisible until it’s too late.
This pack makes it visible in a repeatable monthly process.

## What you get
A client-hosted Power BI dashboard that:
- tracks commission trend (month + YTD)
- shows contribution by provider and adviser
- flags missing ongoing commission signals
- flags statement mismatches, duplicates, and reversals
- provides drilldown to statement line level (traceable)

## Data stays with you
All data remains in your environment. Vantix works with least-privilege access.

## Packages
Starter / Standard / Buy-Out (same tiers as the core kit).

## Boundaries
Standard pack only. Any bespoke work is quoted.

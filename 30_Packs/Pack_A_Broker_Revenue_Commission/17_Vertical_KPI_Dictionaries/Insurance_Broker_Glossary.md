# Insurance Broker Glossary (Quick)

- Commission Statement: Provider-issued statement of commission payable/paid
- Initial Commission: once-off commission on new business
- Ongoing Commission: recurring commission on in-force policies
- Reversal: negative line item reversing prior commission
- Leakage: expected commission not received (or received below expectation)
- Lapse: policy ceases (cancellation/non-payment), often reducing ongoing commission
- Unmatched Line: statement line that cannot be linked to a known policy/adviser/client
